## <img src="https://api.iconify.design/mdi/office-building.svg?color=%234a4a4a" width="24" align="top" />&nbsp; Who We Are

ZOT Engineering Ltd is a **contract electronics manufacturer** specialising in PCB Manufacturing, Electro-Mechanical Assembly, and Sheet Metal Fabrication & Machining. Our **three UK factories** at Inveresk Mills Industrial Park, Musselburgh are equipped with the most advanced technology to offer a complete **turnkey full box-build solution** — all under one roof.

With over **50 years of experience** and a team of **200+ highly skilled engineers and operators**, we are a trusted, reliable partner for Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) across the UK, Europe, North America, and Asia Pacific.

> *"No matter which division of ZOT is best suited to your project, customer service is our priority — trusted to deliver a tailored service that is on time and to budget."*
